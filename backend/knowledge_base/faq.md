# CloudDesk FAQ

## Account & Login

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
**A:**

1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Follow the password reset link sent to your email.

---

**Q: Why am I unable to log in to my account?**
**A:**
Login issues may occur due to incorrect credentials, expired sessions, or disabled browser cookies. Ensure your email and password are correct, clear your browser cache, and enable cookies.

---

**Q: Why is my account locked?**
**A:**
Accounts may be temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes before trying again or reset your password.

---

## Workspace & Users

**Q: How do I invite a team member?**
**A:**

1. Navigate to **Settings → Team Members**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the user's email address.
4. Assign a role such as Admin, Agent, or Viewer.

---

**Q: What roles are available for users?**
**A:**

CloudDesk supports three roles:

* **Admin** – Full access to system settings
* **Agent** – Can manage and respond to tickets
* **Viewer** – Read-only access

---

## Ticket Management

**Q: How do I create a support ticket manually?**
**A:**

1. Click **New Ticket** in the dashboard.
2. Enter the customer’s email address.
3. Add the subject and description of the issue.
4. Submit the ticket.

---

**Q: What ticket statuses exist in CloudDesk?**
**A:**

CloudDesk uses the following ticket statuses:

* **Open** – Ticket has been created
* **Pending** – Waiting for customer reply
* **Resolved** – Issue has been fixed
* **Closed** – Ticket archived

---

**Q: How do I assign a ticket to an agent?**
**A:**
Open the ticket, click **Assign Agent**, and select the appropriate team member.

---

## API & Developer

**Q: Does CloudDesk provide an API?**
**A:**
Yes. CloudDesk provides a REST API that allows developers to integrate ticket creation, management, and customer support workflows into external applications.

---

**Q: How do I generate an API key?**
**A:**

1. Navigate to **Settings → Developer → API Keys**.
2. Click **Generate New Key**.
3. Copy the key and store it securely.

---

**Q: What are the API rate limits?**
**A:**

CloudDesk enforces the following limits:

* **100 requests per minute**
* **5000 requests per day**

Requests exceeding the limit will return **HTTP 429 – Too Many Requests**.

---

## Billing

**Q: What subscription plans does CloudDesk offer?**
**A:**

CloudDesk offers three plans:

* **Starter – $19/month**
* **Pro – $49/month**
* **Enterprise – Custom pricing**

---

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
**A:**

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Update Payment Method**.
3. Enter your new card details and save.

---

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
**A:**
The system retries the payment within 24 hours. If payment remains unsuccessful for 7 days, account access may be restricted.

---

## Integrations

**Q: Can CloudDesk integrate with Slack?**
**A:**
Yes. Slack integration can be enabled via **Settings → Integrations → Slack**.

---

**Q: What integrations are supported?**
**A:**

CloudDesk supports integrations with:

* Slack
* Microsoft Teams
* Zapier
* Jira

---

## Data & Reporting

**Q: How can I export ticket data?**
**A:**

1. Navigate to **Reports → Export Data**.
2. Select a format such as CSV or JSON.
3. Download the report.

---
