# CloudDesk Billing & Subscription

---

## Subscription Plans

CloudDesk offers three plans to suit teams of all sizes.

| Plan       | Price          | Best For                        |
| ---------- | -------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Starter    | $19/month      | Small teams, basic ticketing    |
| Pro        | $49/month      | Growing teams with automation   |
| Enterprise | Custom pricing | Large orgs, advanced analytics  |

All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

---

## What's Included in Each Plan

**Starter – $19/month**
- Up to 3 agents
- Email and web form ticket channels
- Basic ticket management
- 5 GB storage

**Pro – $49/month**
- Up to 20 agents
- All Starter features
- Live chat channel
- Automation rules
- Slack and Zapier integrations
- 25 GB storage

**Enterprise – Custom pricing**
- Unlimited agents
- All Pro features
- Custom SLA policies
- Dedicated account manager
- API access with higher rate limits
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- 99.9% uptime SLA

---

## Free Trial

All new accounts get a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card is required during the trial period. At the end of the trial, the account automatically moves to the Starter plan unless upgraded.

---

## Upgrading or Downgrading

To change your plan:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a new plan and confirm

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrade changes apply at the end of the current billing cycle.

---

## Updating Payment Method

To update your payment information:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Update Payment Method**
3. Enter your new card details
4. Click **Save**

Supported payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal.

---

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
- The system automatically retries within **24 hours**
- A notification email is sent to the billing admin
- If payment remains unsuccessful after **7 days**, some features may be restricted
- After **14 days**, the account may be downgraded to read-only mode

To resolve a failed payment, update your payment method from **Settings → Billing**.

---

## Invoices and Receipts

All invoices are emailed automatically after each successful payment. You can also download past invoices from:

**Settings → Billing → Invoice History**

---

## Refund Policy

CloudDesk does not offer refunds for partial billing cycles. If you cancel your subscription mid-cycle, access continues until the end of the paid period. Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis for technical issues — contact support to request one.

---

## Cancelling a Subscription

To cancel:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation

Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. After that, the account moves to a read-only state. Your data is retained for 30 days before deletion.

---

## Taxes

Applicable taxes are calculated based on your billing address. Tax details appear on each invoice.
