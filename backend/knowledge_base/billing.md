# Billing & Subscription

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- **Credit Cards**: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- **PayPal**: For standard PayPal accounts
- **Wire Transfer**: Available for annual enterprise plans

## Billing Cycle

- **Annual Billing**: Charged once per year with 20% discount

## Common Billing Questions

**How do I update my payment method?**
Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods to add or update your card.

**Can I get a refund?**
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new customers. After 14 days, no refunds are provided but you can cancel anytime.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

**Do you offer discounts?**
Yes! We offer 20% off for annual billing and special pricing for non-profits and startups. Contact sales for details.
